Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2025  ·  Teraiamanu Belanger

This Privacy Policy describes how KanriDiet ("we", "us", or "our"), developed by Teraiamanu Belanger, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the KanriDiet mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address and a username you choose.
  • Health and fitness data: body weight, body fat percentage, and active calories burned (read from Apple HealthKit with your permission); meal logs, calorie and macro nutrient data you enter or that is generated from photos you take.
  • Photos: photos of food you choose to upload for AI-powered meal analysis. These are stored securely and used solely to identify nutritional content.
  • Approximate location: city/state-level location used to provide relevant food database results.
  • Usage data: anonymized data about how you interact with the App (screen views, feature usage), collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device identifiers: used for push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's core features (meal logging, nutrition tracking, recipe management).
  • To calculate personalized nutrition targets based on your profile and health data.
  • To enable the optional Coach feature, where a coach you connect with can view your meal logs.
  • To send you push notifications relevant to your usage (meal reminders, coach messages).
  • To improve the App through anonymized analytics.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers provided by Supabase, Inc. (United States). By using the App, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit. Supabase applies encryption at rest.

4. Health Data (APPI — Japan)

Health and fitness data constitutes "special care-required personal information" under Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI). We collect and use this data only with your explicit consent, solely for the purpose of providing nutrition tracking features within the App. We do not sell, share, or disclose this data to third parties except as described in this policy.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Supabase – database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Google Firebase – push notifications and analytics (privacy policy)
  • Google Gemini API – AI-powered food photo analysis (images are processed server-side and not retained)
  • USDA FoodData Central – nutrition reference data (public database, no personal data shared)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With service providers listed above, to operate the App.
  • With a coach you explicitly connect with, limited to your meal logs.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the App (Settings → Account → Delete Account) or by contacting us at kanridiet.support@gmail.com. Data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Japanese users have rights under APPI. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kanridiet.support@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
